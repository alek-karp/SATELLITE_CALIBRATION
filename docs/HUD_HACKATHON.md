# HUD Frontier/RSI RL Environments Hackathon

## Overview

**Application Deadline:** June 13, 2026

HUD (W25) and YC are co-hosting the first RSI hackathon, a technical weekend focused on frontier RL environments, post-training datasets, evals, and RFT workflows.

RL environments are one of the best ways to improve frontier model capabilities.

The future is possible. We need to teach models how to get there.

Try to imagine what the world of 2040 looks like, then work backwards.

## Core Idea

You can improve models at anything you can verify.

The only question left to answer is:

**What will you teach them?**

## What Teams Should Build

Teams are encouraged to build environments and agents that push model capabilities in areas such as:

- Coding

- Autonomous research

## Why Join

Participants will get to:

- Spend 24 hours collaborating and competing with top builders from San Francisco and beyond
- Build state-of-the-art agents, evals, and post-training data
- Learn and use Reinforcement Learning and Reinforcement Fine-Tuning
- Get hands-on experience with tools from frontier technology companies
- Hear directly from industry leaders about what they are building and what they have learned
